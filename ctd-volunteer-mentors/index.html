Thank You CTD Volunteers

We rely on your support. From classes to practicum projects, to job readiness skills, to our social enterprise apprenticeship, Code the Dream’s many dedicated volunteers make our innovative program possible. In 2020, dozens of volunteers gave more than 1100 hours of time supporting our students and interns. Thank you so much to all our volunteers! We couldn’t do it without you, and we are so grateful!

Want to join their ranks? Check out our volunteer opportunities and sign up!
